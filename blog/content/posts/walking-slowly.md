title: Walking slowly
category: poetry
tags: poetry, personal-thoughts

<pre>
I walk slowly through this starry night,
But I’m really speeding through space and time.
The slowness is an illusion,
     but it's all I can understand;
That there is an end ahead,
     just doesn’t work in my head.
</pre>
